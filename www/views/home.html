Project Amare

Connecting patients and families around the world

Project Amare is a network that aims to connect patients or family members of patients with other individuals who have gone through the same disease, and are inspired to share their knowledge about this disease. These individuals are our "champions." Amare aims to ultimately empower patients, and provide for them a source of knowledge and emotional support.

Register now »

How does it work?

  • Create an account
  • Find a "Champion" -- an individual who has gone through a disease and can provide information and insight.
  • Message the "champion" to introduce yourself.
  • If appropriate, continue messaging and/or calling the "champion."
  • Post questions to our community on disease-specific forums.
  • Find other "champions" for additional insights.
  • Check out our article posts for the newest research.

What makes Amare different:

One-on-one

One-on-one, personal, continued relationships with multiple "champions."

Connection

Connection with enthusiastic, savvy individuals or "champions" who can provide you with insightful info

Personalized

Personalized feeds with information about the latest research, specific to disease, generated by champions and by our undergraduate research team.

Voice and Video

Both voice/video call and messaging are available as ways of communication.
